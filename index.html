The economic effect of the MWC on Barcelona.

A visual analysis of credit card transactions before and during Mobile World Congress to reveal the economic impact on the city.

The data used in this visualization corresponds to transactions done via BBVA credit cards or commerce with BBVA terminals. It compares historical data from the week before and during the MWC. The data has been wholly anonymized, and includes only where and when transactions were performed, and DOES NOT include any personal information, in accordance with Spanish LOPD law.
